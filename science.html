🧪 THE SCIENTIFIC METHOD

The foundation of all scientific discovery

1

🔍 OBSERVATION

Observe the world around you. Notice something interesting or puzzling. What do you see? What patterns exist?

2

❓ QUESTION

Ask a testable question about your observation. A good question is specific, measurable, and answerable through experimentation.

3

💡 HYPOTHESIS

Form a prediction (educated guess) based on your knowledge. Written as "If _____, then _____, because _____."

4

🧫 EXPERIMENT

Design and conduct a controlled experiment. Change only one variable at a time (independent variable) and measure the effect (dependent variable).

5

📊 ANALYZE DATA

Collect and record data. Create graphs and tables. Look for patterns, trends, and relationships in your results.

6

✅ CONCLUSION

Did your results support your hypothesis? What did you learn? How can this knowledge be applied or investigated further?
